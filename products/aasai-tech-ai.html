Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

🤖

Real-time AI responses

Intelligent, context-aware answers to any question — writing, coding, research, math, or general knowledge. Understands multi-turn conversations within a session.

⏱️

Streaming replies

For signed-in users, responses stream word-by-word in real time — like watching the AI think — for a smooth, natural experience.

🖼️

Image understanding (Vision AI)

Attach up to 4 images per message (10 MB each), or paste from the clipboard. The AI reads, describes, extracts text from, and compares images.

🔒

Secure Google Sign-In

One-click sign-in via Google Identity Services. No passwords, no forms.

📂

Cloud-synced history

Signed-in users have every conversation saved to the cloud and accessible from any device. Delete individual chats or export everything as JSON.

👤

Guest mode

Anyone can start chatting instantly without an account. Guest chats are temporary and subject to a message limit.

📊

Usage statistics dashboard

A live panel showing total messages, tokens consumed, sessions, today’s activity, and a quota progress bar — plus a per-session token breakdown.

🎨

Customisation & personalisation

Pick a custom accent colour or one of 10 presets, upload a custom profile avatar, and tune behaviour like Enter-to-send and the typing indicator.

📱

Mobile & desktop ready

Fully responsive with an overlay sidebar on small screens and iOS safe-area handling, plus keyboard shortcuts (Cmd/Ctrl+K, Enter, Esc).
