How it works

Live in four
simple steps

Step 01
🪪

Create your agent

Name your agent, upload your logo, choose a brand colour and theme, write a welcome message, pick a type (Support, Sales, FAQ, General), and set a system prompt to define its behaviour.

⚡ No code needed
Step 02
📚

Add your knowledge base

Paste FAQ text, upload PDF/DOCX/TXT files, or enter a webpage URL to scrape. SupportGent extracts, indexes, and learns your business content.

⚡ Smart training
Step 03
🛍️

(Optional) Enable shopping

Toggle shopping on, add products manually or via CSV, connect your Razorpay keys, and set your currency to accept payments inside the chat.

⚡ Sales-ready
Step 04
🚀

Publish & connect channels

Publish to an instant subdomain or embed one HTML line. Connect WhatsApp, Instagram (Meta), and Gmail, and enable AI auto-reply per channel.

⚡ Go live

Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

🧱

No-code AI agent builder

Set agent name and persona, upload a logo and welcome message, write a custom system prompt, choose dark/light theme and brand colour, with a real-time live preview.

🧠

Knowledge base (smart training)

Train on text/FAQ, PDF/DOCX/TXT uploads, and URL scraping. Add multiple knowledge sources per agent — scaling with your plan — with token tracking per source.

🛒

Shopping & sales automation

Product catalogue (manual or CSV), AI product recommendations, Razorpay in-chat payments, order management (Pending/Paid/Fulfilled/Refunded), and sales analytics.

📡

Multi-channel support

Website widget (one line of HTML), WhatsApp Business, Instagram DMs, and Gmail/email — with an AI auto-reply toggle per channel so you stay in control.

📨

Unified conversations inbox

View, search, and manage every conversation from all channels in one feed, with AI-reply labels and real-time updates — no page refresh.

🌍

Multi-language UI

🌐

Publishing & deployment

Dashboard & analytics

Conversation stats with 14-day trends, a token usage meter vs. your plan cap, an agent overview with share links, and full billing history.
