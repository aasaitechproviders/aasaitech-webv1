The problem

Real problems, solved

🏫

Schools can’t afford custom software

Most schools share a generic platform or spend lakhs building their own. SmartEDU gives every school a fully white-labelled portal on its own subdomain at a fraction of the cost.

🌙

No one to ask at 10 PM

Doubt-clearing is limited to school hours. With SmartEDU’s AI assistant, a student can ask any question in their language and get an instant, structured answer any time.

📂

Teachers waste hours distributing notes

Teachers juggle WhatsApp groups, email, and USB drives. SmartEDU gives a drag-and-drop upload panel that instantly makes materials available and feeds the AI.

👁️

Admins are blind to portal activity

Without a console, admins can’t see usage or enrolment. The Admin Dashboard gives real-time visibility into accounts, uploads, and statistics.

🤖

Generic AI doesn’t know your curriculum

Public tools answer from the open internet. SmartEDU’s RAG engine retrieves answers exclusively from the documents your own teachers uploaded — always curriculum-aligned.

How it works

Live in four
simple steps

Step 01
🏫

School onboarding

Aasai registers the school, creates a subdomain (e.g. aasaischool.ai.aasaitech.in), and uploads the school’s logo, name, and brand colours. No installation required.

⚡ Zero setup for schools
Step 02
👥

Admin sets up users

The admin logs in via Google Sign-In and adds teacher emails through the Admin Dashboard. Teachers get instant access; students self-register or are added in bulk.

⚡ Minutes
Step 03
📤

Teachers upload materials

Teachers select the class standard and subject, then upload PDFs, notes, or worksheets. Each document is processed by AWS Lambda and indexed into the RAG knowledge base.

⚡ Drag & drop
Step 04
💬

Students ask questions

Students pick standard, subject, and preferred language, then type a question. The AI retrieves relevant passages and returns a structured answer — with tables, derivations, or step-by-step solutions.

⚡ Instant

Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

🎨

Multi-tenant school branding

The system detects the school from its subdomain and loads its name, logo, and custom colours across the entire portal. Everyone feels they’re on their own school’s platform.

🔑

Google Sign-In authentication

Login is handled entirely through Google OAuth 2.0 — no usernames or passwords. Users are mapped to a school and role and routed to the right dashboard automatically.

🧭

Role-based dashboards

Three distinct dashboards — Admin, Teacher, and Student — each with its own interface, permissions, and tools. No clutter from features meant for other roles.

📤

Teacher document upload portal

Drag-and-drop upload, standard/subject categorisation, group-level targeting, real-time progress, a personal library with status, and total page-count tracking.

🧠

AI student Q&A (RAG engine)

Students select standard, subject, medium and answer language, ask a question, and get a structured answer sourced only from the school’s own materials — never hallucinated.

📊

Admin dashboard

Live stats (documents, processing status, pages indexed), teacher and student management, a document activity log with timestamps, and search across users.

🔐

Session management & security

JWT tokens silently restore valid sessions; role verification is enforced on every dashboard, redirecting unauthorised users to login.

📱

Responsive, mobile-friendly

All dashboards work on phones, tablets, and desktops — ideal for Indian students who often use smartphones.
