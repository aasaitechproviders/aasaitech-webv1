The problem

Real problems, solved

📦

High return rates

Customers order, discover the outfit doesn’t suit them, and return it — costing time, logistics, and restocking effort.

🐢

Slow buying decisions

"Will this saree match my skin tone? Does this blouse fit?" Every doubt over WhatsApp delays or kills the sale.

🪞

No visual confidence

Product photos show garments on a mannequin or generic model — not on the actual customer. Trust and excitement are lost.

🧑‍💻

No app, no tech budget

Boutique owners aren’t developers. They can’t build or afford a custom try-on app — they need something that works from day one.

🌙

After-hours sales lost

When a customer browses at 10pm with questions, there’s no one to answer. Potential sales slip away overnight.

🔗

Low upselling

Showing how a saree, blouse, and jewellery work together is hard over chat. Boutiques lose combo and accessory sales.

How it works

From zero to live
in three steps

Step 01
🛍️

Create your agent

Sign in with Google. Enter your boutique name, choose your category (Sarees, Western Wear, Jewellery…), pick a brand colour, and write a welcome message. Your branded AI stylist is created instantly.

⚡ Sign in with Google
Step 02
📸

Upload your catalog

Upload product photos — sarees, dresses, jewellery, footwear, or any apparel. The AI learns your inventory automatically. Add or remove products anytime; changes go live immediately.

⚡ Drag & drop
Step 03
🔗

Share your link & go live

Copy your unique try-on URL and share it on WhatsApp, Instagram bio, or your website. Customers tap, upload a selfie, pick an outfit, and see their try-on right in the browser.

⚡ Done. You're live.

Real-world use cases

How BoutiqueAI solves
real problems

01

WhatsApp Boutique — closing sales faster

A saree store gets a steady stream of WhatsApp enquiries, but many shoppers hesitate because they’re unsure how a piece will look on them. With a try-on link on every catalog message, customers upload a photo, try the saree, and reply ready to order — shortening the back-and-forth.

02

Instagram Boutique — nationwide sales

A western-wear boutique ships across India via Instagram DMs. The bio link lets anyone try on any piece from anywhere, so shoppers buy with more confidence after seeing the outfit on themselves, and the AI handles common "do you have size M?" questions around the clock.

03

Jewellery Store — cross-selling with confidence

A jewellery brand uses combo try-ons so customers pick a saree and a necklace together and see the complete look in one image — making it natural to bundle matching sets and lift the average order.

04

Kids Wear — reducing returns

A kids clothing store sees returns from catalog-based ordering. Parents upload a photo of their child, pick a frock or ethnic outfit, and see a try-on before ordering — helping reduce returns and build repeat trust.

Security & privacy

Customer data is safe

🔒

Encrypted image storage

All customer photos are stored in encrypted AWS S3 buckets. Images are never shared or used for AI model training.

🗑️

Customer deletion rights

Customers can delete their base photo at any time from within the try-on interface.

💳

Secure payments via Razorpay

Payments are processed through Razorpay (PCI-DSS compliant). Card details are never stored on BoutiqueAI servers.

🔐

Google OAuth login

Boutique owner accounts are secured with Google Sign-In. No passwords to manage or forget.
